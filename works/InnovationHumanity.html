Manage an innovative project

During this course, we learned about the innovation method that is the TRIZ. TRIZ is a method bringing together a set of tools for inventive problem solving. We studied an everyday object and applied the TRIZ method to it in order to have a better idea of ​​how innovations work.

For the completed project, I chose the toothbrush and made an S-curve, a functional diagram and established the 9 laws of the TRIZ to this object.

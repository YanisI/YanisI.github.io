Who am I ?

My name is Yanis Imekraz, I am 22 years old and I am a 5th year student at INSA in Toulouse. I obtained a DUT in computer science and electronics in 2018. Following this, I continued in engineering school in computer science and network before moving into the last year in Innovative Smart System.

During this DUT I did an internship at the Institute of Electronics and Systems on a conditioner for hot wire anemometers. I did this internship independently and this internship allowed me to apply my knowledge in electronic design and routing on a real project.

Subsequently, I entered INSA, in computer science and network, before choosing Innovative Smart System as a specialty. I was able to develop through many projects as part of the training my spirit and teamwork. I developed skills in several programming languages ​​such as Java, C, VHDL ... I then followed an internship at LAAS also independently. During this internship I participated in the design of a set of algorithms for logical diagnostics. I was also introduced to the C ++ language as well as the data format in JSON.
